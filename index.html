  • Reduces inflammation: Sea moss contains anti-inflammatory compounds that can help reduce inflammation throughout the body.
  • Increases energy levels: Sea moss is high in iron, which can help increase energy levels and reduce fatigue.
  • Promotes healthy skin, hair, and nails: Sea moss contains collagen, which is important for healthy skin, hair, and nails.
